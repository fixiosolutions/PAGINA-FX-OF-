### Key Constraints Avoided:
- ❌ **No features on the map:** Triggers are kept strictly to psychological behaviors, needs, hopes, and fears. Features (e.g. "Wi-Fi connection") are mapped separately in the Feature Impact Analysis, not in the core psychological map.

- **Vision:** E-commerce for practical tools (Mascotas, Conectividad, Hogar, Carro) resolving daily problems, built with a premium, non-IA custom look, direct support/warranty in Bogota, and mandatory login for purchases and forum interaction.

- **Pilot Product:** IMIPAW Smart Feeder ($250.000 COP).
- **Secondary Products:** Portable jump starter (USB-C), portable tire inflator.
- **Success Criteria:** 5 feeders/month sold, 6.7% review rate (1:15), 100% transactional success.

## Layer 3: Domain Research

### 1. Smart Pet Feeders (IMIPAW):
- *Key Frustrations:* 2.4GHz Wi-Fi network splitting issues during pairing; fear of food jamming (irregular kibble shapes); fear of device disconnecting during power/Wi-Fi outages (need battery backup); pets hacking or opening lids.
- *Key Motivations:* Diet portion control to prevent vomiting/obesity; food freshness (hermetic seals); peace of mind when working late or in traffic.

### 2. Portable Jump Starters & Inflators:
- *Key Frustrations:* Battery self-discharging in the trunk (dead when needed); inflators overheating during heavy inflation; fake "peak amp" ratings; short/flimsy clamps.
- *Key Motivations:* Roadside safety and independence (especially at night or in dangerous zones in Bogota); quick 3-minute battery jump; ease of charging via phone's USB-C charger.
